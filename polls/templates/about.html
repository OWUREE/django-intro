ABOUT OUR SYSTEM

By Owuri Hanifat, David Baba Madaki

It all started some 5 months ago when my partner David Baba Madaki and i Owuri Hanifat decided to come up with a solid waste management (SWM) system as our project topic.

The systems website was designed using the Python Django framework, as well as the Django API, and its composed of 5 major routes:

  • Home Page/ Dashboard
  • About Page.
  • Register Page.
  • Login page.
  • Admin Page

About page!!

The solid waste management system was designed to tackle the issue of improper waste disposal, and waste of useful resources.

useful resources in this case means such waste which could be reused but are rather thrown away.

  • in order to ensure that good quality of waste products are retained for recyling, we decided to develop a SYSTEM, capable of segreagating waste at the sourc level by seperating:

    • metals.
    • plastics.
    • from others.

  • To prevent pollution resulting from overflowing of waste bin, the system was incorporated with a level sensor.

  • Also, the system was able to optimize resources by conserving power, fuel etc.

  • and finally, we can hope for a clean and hygienic environment.
